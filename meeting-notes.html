Meeting Notes - November 1998

[[BEGINNING TRANSCRIPTION OF MEETING...]]

MERCER: Alright, good. Now that everyone's here, we can begin.

MERCER: Felix, tell me what the submersible found.

[SHUFFLING OF PAPERS]

NARVÁEZ: Uh, well... images from the submersible initially showed a normal deep-sea ecosystem -- shrimp here and there, isopods...

NARVÁEZ: We even got a nice picture of an elephant seal--

MERCER: The seal isn't why we're here.

NARVÁEZ: Right. Elias.

PETROV: The submersible threw up warnings and errors after forty-five minutes of exploration.

PETROV: A huge chunk was missing from it upon retrieval.

NARVÁEZ: Initially, I assumed it might've been a hungry or even curious colossal squid.

PETROV: There were teeth marks, though.

MERCER: Teeth marks?

NARVÁEZ: Which led me to hypothesize it could have bumped into a sperm whale; however, the teeth marks don't match.

[HUM OF FLUORESCENT LIGHTS]

[MERCER SIGHS]

[SLOW CLICKING OF SHOES AGAINST TILE]

MERCER: Was there any photo taken at the time of the incident?

NARVÁEZ: One.

[SHUFFLING OF PAPERS]

[PAPER BEING SLID ACROSS TABLE]

[FOOTSTEPS STOP]

[HUM OF FLUORESCENT LIGHTS]

MERCER: What the hell am I looking at?

NARVÁEZ: Maybe... a new species of sperm whale, or some new animal entirely--

PETROV: Whatever it is, it's like it knew exactly where to bite to completely fuck up the internal mechanisms.

MERCER: We'll conduct more research. Figure out exactly what this is.

MERCER: Elias, go ahead and fix the submersible as best you can. If you can't, just let us know. We have, what -- two or three others left?

PETROV: Correct.

MERCER: Felix, we'll circle back with the others on this. Let them know what happened and what the next steps are.

MERCER: ORCA, finish meeting notes.

[[ENDING TRANSCRIPTION OF MEETING...]]
